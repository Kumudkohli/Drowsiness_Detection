DEEP DROWSINESS DETECTION

In the context of auto accidents, drowsiness detection is essential for averting potentially disastrous events brought on by driver sleepiness. Fatigue raises the risk of accidents greatly because it inhibits cognitive function, slows reaction times, and degrades decision-making skills. Detection systems have the ability to quickly recognize indicators of fatigue, such as drooping eyelids or irregular steering, and can notify drivers to pull over or take breaks, thereby reducing the likelihood of accidents and saving lives while driving.
